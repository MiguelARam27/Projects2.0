NOMINEE’S ROLE

North Terminal Development Program Director, Miami Dade Aviation Department

CHALLENGE

This complex $3.1 billion, 3.8-million-square-foot project aimed to expand and renovate Concourses A-E at Miami International Airport into a modern 1.3-mile linear terminal, cementing the airport’s role as “Gateway to the Americas.”

OUTCOME

Plagued with cost overruns and delays, the project was in crisis when JC took over as program director. He led the team in reworking drawings and rephasing to achieve the design intention and operational goals, while maintaining 24/7 function at 37 gates during construction.


The strategy shaved more than a year off the schedule and saved more than $300 million. JC managed a project team of more than 100 companies and personally designed, prepared and signed/sealed construction documents for more than $120 million of work. The terminal design reflects the open arms of the city and employs sustainable standards. Every milestone was achieved on or ahead of schedule.
